Pressure Management Services

  • Iron Inspection &Recertification Services
  • Your high-pressure iron is mission critical. Our detailed inspection process helps detect and eliminate defects, fatigue, and potential points of failure — ensuring the safety of your team, your equipment, and the environment. Regular inspections are essential to avoid costly non-productive time (NPT) and to comply with industry safety regulations.
  • We offer hydrotesting and ND inspections through fully equipped service trailers, ensuring maximum convenience and consistency across all locations.
  • All work is performed in strict adherence to OEM specifications and international standards (e.g., API,ASME, NACE), providing full compliance and traceability.
  • Our Services Include:
  • Visual Inspection
    Ultrasonic Testing (UT)
    Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)
    Magnetic Particle Inspection (MPI)
    Thread & Connection
    Cleaning
    Banding
    Rattle Testing
    Rebuild & Repair to OEM
    Specifications
    Hydrostatic Pressure Testing
    Sandblast & Paint
    Finishing
  • Equipment We Inspect, Test & Recertify
  • Iron Equipment (Flow Iron/ Treating Iron)
    Types: Pup joints, swivel
    joints, hammer unions, manifolds, plug valves, check valves, tees, crosses
    Standards: API 6A, API 16C, NACE MR0175
    Testing: Hydrostatic pressure, NDT (MPI/UT), dimensional & visual inspection
  • Blowout Preventers (BOPs)
    Types: Annular BOPs, Ram BOPs(pipe rams, blind rams, shear rams)
    Standard: API 16A
    Services: Low/high-pressure testing, sealing integrity & function testing
  • Choke & Kill Manifolds
    Components: Valves, chokes, spools, flanges, gauges
    Services: Valve seat testing, hydrotesting, NDT, full documentation
  • High-Pressure Valves
    Types: Gate, plug, ball valves
    Applications: Wellheads, fracking, flowback
    Services: Pressure testing, NDT, seat inspection
  • Wellhead Equipment
    Includes: Casing/tubing heads, crosses, tees, hangers
    Services: Thread inspection, dimensional checks, pressure testing
  • Pressure Control Equipment
    Examples: Frack trees, flowback assemblies, isolation tools
    Services: Full pressure and function testing, MPI
  • Coiled Tubing & Wireline Pressure Control Equipment
    Includes: Stuffing boxes, lubricators, quick test subs, wireline BOPs
    Services: Leak and pressure testing before every job
  • Surface Pressure Vessels & Tanks
    Examples: Accumulator bottles, surge tanks, separators
    Standards: ASME Section VIII, API 510
    Services: Internal inspections, hydrostatic testing, corrosion checks
  • Hydraulic Accumulators& Control Panels
    Services: Bladder integrity checks, calibration, hydraulic system testing
  • Hoses & Flexible Lines
    Types: Cement hoses, choke& kill lines, hydraulic hoses
    Services: Burst testing, end-connection inspection, requalification
